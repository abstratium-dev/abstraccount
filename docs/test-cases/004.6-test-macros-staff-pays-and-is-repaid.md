# Test Case 004.6: Test Macros - PaymentByStaff and RepayStaff

**Feature:** Macro System - PaymentByStaff and RepayStaff Macros  
**Date:** 2026-04-06  

## Preconditions

See [004.1-test-macros.md](./004.1-test-macros.md) for general macro testing preconditions.

Additionally:
- Partner P00000014 must exist (supplier)
- Partner P00000001 must exist (staff member)
- Account 6570.001 must exist (expense account)
- Account 2210.001 must exist (staff member liability account)
- Account 1020 must exist (bank account)

## Test Objective

Verify that the PaymentByStaff and RepayStaff macros work together to:
1. Record expenses paid by staff members on behalf of the company
2. Repay staff members for those expenses

The test will validate:
- PaymentByStaff macro creates a liability to the staff member
- RepayStaff macro clears that liability
- Proper account filtering and parameter validation
- Transaction creation and balance updates

## Notes

- **PaymentByStaff** is used when a staff member pays an expense on behalf of the company
  - Creates a liability to the staff member (company owes them money)
  - Debits the expense account (increases expense)
  - Credits the staff liability account (increases liability)
  - Example: Staff member pays a supplier invoice for CHF 17.00

- **RepayStaff** is used to repay money owed to staff members
  - Clears the liability created by PaymentByStaff
  - Debits the staff liability account (reduces liability)
  - Credits the bank account (cash goes out)
  - Example: Repaying the staff member for the CHF 17.00 they paid

- This test validates the complete cycle:
  1. Staff pays supplier (creates liability)
  2. Company repays staff (clears liability)

- Invoice numbers should reference the original invoices being reimbursed
- The {next_invoice_PI} placeholder auto-generates the next purchase invoice number
